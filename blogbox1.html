Best Guitars To Buy For Beginners

Have you decided to start learning the guitar? But have no idea how to pick the right one for you. Well, if that’s the case, you’ve come to the right place! It’s 2022 and there are tons of great guitars out there, but choosing the right instrument can be super stressful. This is exactly why BAJAAO brings to you a quick buying guide for some of the best beginner guitars available for you in 2022. Let’s jump right in! We’re going to start this list off with a few of the most loved guitars by beginners all around the world.

Yamaha F310

Yamaha's reputation for quality ensures that the F310 produces a big tone with plenty of sustain as well as being easy to play. It comes with a beautiful Spruce Top, Meranti Back and Sides, a Nato neck and a Rosewood Fretboard. This is arguably the most popular acoustic guitar for beginners across the globe.

Cort AD-810

The Cort AD 810 is another guitar which delivers impeccable performance and tone. This one also comes with a premium tonewood selection featuring a Spruce Top along with Mahogany Back and Sides. The Cort AD810 Dreadnought Acoustic Guitar meets the needs of fingerstyle players and flatpickers alike. It helps musicians expand their creative horizons by letting them experiment with different musical genres.

Fender SA 150C

No list of guitars is complete without a Fender Lineup and the SA series by fender is great for anyone starting to learn the mighty instrument. They have a Hardwood / Lindenwood top and both feature Mahogany Back and Sides. They both also feature a Stained Hardwood Fretboard. We’ve tried to include a bit of something for everyone here, and you can rest assured, all of these guitars have been tried and tested by us, and beginners all around the world.
